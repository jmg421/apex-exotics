Reading the Chart:
█ Green Candle: Price went UP (close > open)
█ Red Candle: Price went DOWN (close < open)
│ Wicks: High/low reached during that 10-second period
┄ Yellow Line: Your entry price (what you paid)
▼ Doji: Tiny body, long wicks = indecision
▼ Hammer: Long lower wick = potential bounce
▼ Shooting Star: Long upper wick = potential drop
▼ Engulfing: Big candle swallows previous = momentum shift
Learning patterns: Watch how they form in real-time. Notice how often they appear vs. how rarely price actually reverses. The truth: Patterns are fun to spot, but your edge is finding overlooked companies with strong fundamentals, not predicting short-term moves.
